LSD - Little Sticky Destroyer

What is LSD?

LSD is a physics based volleyball game written in Lua using the Löve framework. It is currently under development.

How to play?

Shoot the ball onto the grey platforms. As you leave a platform, it will explode - the goal is to destroy all of them. Avoid the red walls, they'll kill you! When you start, you better be fast, the "Timer of Death©®™" is at the bottom!

Controls

  • Shoot: "LEFT MOUSE BUTTON"
  • Retry: "R" , "ENTER" or "SPACE"
  • Exit / Menu: "ESCAPE"
